Chapter 01 — Abstract
What is HappyHouseToken?
HappyHouseToken ($HHT) is a community-driven meme coin built on the Solana blockchain, launched through pump.fun. Unlike typical meme coins that serve a single community, $HHT is designed to be the home for all meme coin communities — a shared space where DOGE holders, PEPE fans, SHIB enthusiasts, and every other meme coin community can come together as neighbors.
Chapter 02 — Vision & Mission
The HappyHouse Philosophy
The crypto space has given us thousands of meme coins. Each with their own community, their own culture, their own jokes. But they all live in isolation. DOGE has a street. PEPE has a pond. SHIB has a yard. But every meme coin needs a real home. HappyHouseToken is that home — a place where the entire meme coin universe can gather, play, build and thrive together.
Chapter 06 — Tokenomics
Fair, Simple & Transparent
$HHT launched with a total supply of 1,000,000,000 tokens. There is zero team allocation, zero pre-mine, and zero insider advantage. Every token was made available to the public through pump.fun's fair launch mechanism. The token will migrate to Raydium DEX automatically upon reaching a $69,000 market cap, providing full liquidity and decentralized trading for all holders.
